How Does Free Trial Work?

Our free trial is different from other companies. From onboarding thousands of customers, we know every company does inventory management differently. As a result, we provide training and consulting with each 7-day trial.

During your free trial, you will be assigned a customer relationship manager who will provide recommendations on how best to apply RedRock Inventory Management System to your inventory management needs and train you to be empowered with the software.

Why Is My Credit Card Needed?
To ensure our relationship managers devote time to customers who are ‘serious’ in evaluating and using our software, we ask for a payment method as a way to identify them and ensure your subscription is renewed after the free trial.

Please Note that your card will never be charged during your free trial except for a token of ₦50 during registration to enable our payment processors validate your card and ensure it is functional. The ₦50 token will be refunded immediately depending on your bank.

When Will RedRock Technologies Ltd Charge My Credit Card?
During registration you select a billing duration, but every new account is on a 7 day free trial so you don’t get charged until after 7 days if you do not cancel your account. You can cancel your account by letting your customer relationship manager know and we’ll not charge your card when the free trial ends. Please note that we will NEVER charge your card until we receive your permission.
